Mary Elizabeth Bowser was born in the year 1839 in Richmond, Virginia as a slave. She was a slave to John Van Lew, who was a wealthy hardware merchant. When John Van Lew died in the year 1851, his daughter, Elizabeth Van Lew, freed all the slaves on her father's plantation. Even though Elizabeth freed all the slaves, Mary decided to stay with the Van Lew family.
